clean.py has course catalog web scrapper

tagger.py has all the tagging stuff

data folder should be self explanatory

tag_list.json has permanent tags, which are tags that are not dependent on enrollment like seats available and time slots
will work on dynamic tags later

the current tags used are:
division(ie lower/upper)
graduate level(undergraduate/graduate)
DEI
Difficulty (GPA>3.3 easy, 3.3>GPA>2.3 medium, 2.3>GPA hard)
no prerequisites
Department code(ie BENG or MATH)
Department name(ie bioengineering or mathematics)
